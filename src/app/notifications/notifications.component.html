Notifications:~

1. Why should I set up a Price Alert?

Because we all love cheap flights! Flight prices or Travels prices, like it or not, tend to change very quickly with time. When you set up a Price Alert, whenever the price of your flight goes up, or down, you get a notification. This helps you find the ideal date and time when to book your flight and get the best price.

2. How do I set up a Price Alert for my flight?

Whenever the price of your selected flight changes, we will instantly send you an email indicating the change in price.

3. How many Price Alerts can I create?

As many as you like! You can track multiple flight prices by creating multiple Price Alerts.

4. What really is the best time to book my flight?

We wish there was a simple answer, flights prices are dependent on a lot of factors, seasonality, day of your flights and buses, how many seats have already been booked and popularity of the route to name a few! When you sign up for Price Alerts, you can easily monitor changes in the prices of flights you track, and book when you feel the price is right.
